What is doctor home healthcare?

Our home healthcare service brings professional medical care directly to your residence in Dubai. We provide comprehensive services including routine checkups, diagnosis and treatment of illnesses, post-operative care, chronic disease management, and more. This service is ideal for:

  • Busy professionals who can't take time off work
  • Elderly patients who find travel difficult
  • Parents with young children
  • Patients recovering from surgery or illness
  • Those who prefer the comfort and privacy of home

All services are provided by licensed physicians with the same quality of care you'd receive in a clinic setting.

Are your doctors licensed in the UAE?

Absolutely. All our physicians are fully licensed by the Dubai Health Authority (DHA) or the Ministry of Health and Prevention (MOHAP) to practice medicine in the UAE. We maintain rigorous standards for our medical team:

  • All doctors have valid UAE medical licenses
  • Minimum 5 years of clinical experience
  • Specialty certifications where applicable
  • Regular training on UAE healthcare protocols
  • Background checks and credential verification

You can request to see your doctor's credentials at the time of your appointment if you wish.

How quickly can a doctor arrive at our home?

Our standard response times are:

  • Regular appointments: Within 2 hours (typically 60-90 minutes)
  • Priority service: Within 45 minutes (additional fee applies)
  • Emergency cases: Immediate dispatch with continuous coordination

Actual arrival time depends on:

  1. Your location in Dubai (we cover all areas)
  2. Current traffic conditions
  3. Time of day (peak hours may affect travel time)
  4. Doctor availability at the time of booking

We provide real-time updates on your doctor's estimated arrival time after booking.
